12.4 Choosing the Right Kind of Loop

Generally, for-loops are what you want to use to iterate over a sequence. Both for-loops and while-loops are appropriate for simple counting loops. You may prefer using for-loops with ranges for counting purposes because it requires less typing than the equivalent while-loop. For most other non-counting loops that have complicated loop conditions and/or donâ€™t involve iterating over sequences, while-loops are likely the best choice.
